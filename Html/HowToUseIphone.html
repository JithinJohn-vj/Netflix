How to use Netflix on your iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch


Learn about Netflix features on iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch, including how to set up and sign out of your account.


The Netflix app is available on iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch models running iOS 16 or later, and is compatible with devices running iOS 5 or later that have already downloaded the Netflix app. Instructions for determining which iOS version you have can be found on the Apple support site.


NOTE: Devices running iOS 5 or later may be able to download an earlier version of the Netflix app if the app was downloaded in the past. See the Apple support site for instructions.


To connect your iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch to your Netflix account, make sure you are on the Home screen and follow the steps below.

  1. Open the App Store.
  2. Select Search.
  3. Type Netflix in the Search bar and tap the Search button.
  4. Select the Netflix search result.
  5. Select Get or tap the Cloud icon.
    • If you are missing the option to install the Netflix app, follow these troubleshooting steps.
  6. Tap the Netflix icon on the Home screen.
  7. Enter your Netflix email address and password.
  8. Select Sign In.

Your device is now connected to your Netflix account.

Netflix streaming features for supported devices include:

Search

Find TV shows and movies by searching directly on the Netflix app when using your iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch.

Subtitles & alternate audio

While a TV show or movie is playing, tap the screen to bring up your play options. On the right you'll find an option to enable subtitles, if available.

Downloads

Download TV shows and movies to watch offline on iOS 9 or later.

Video out

iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch models support video out via a number of compatible Apple-specific connection kits. More information is available through the Apple support site.

Picture in Picture (requires iOS 14)

Certain iPad and iPhone models allow you to watch Netflix while using other apps. For detailed instructions on using Picture in Picture, visit the Apple support site.

Spatial Audio (requires iOS 14.6 or later)

The Netflix Premium plan supports spatial audio on compatible iPad and iPhone models using AirPods Pro and AirPods Max headphones. For detailed instructions on using spatial audio, visit the Apple support site.

NOTE:Spatial audio is supported when streaming content. Downloaded TV shows and movies will use stereo or binaural audio.

High Dynamic Range

Netflix is available in High Dynamic Range (HDR) on select devices. Review the compatible models section to see if HDR works on your device. See our article about HDR10 and Dolby Vision to learn more.

NOTE:Devices must run the latest version of iOS for Dolby Vision playback to work properly.

There are 2 ways to sign out of Netflix on your iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch:

  1. In the lower right, tap My Netflix.
  2. In the upper right, tap More more menu icon.
  3. Tap Sign Out sign out icon, then tap Sign Out to confirm.
  1. Swipe up from the bottom of the screen and pause to show currently open apps.
    • For devices with a Home button, press twice quickly.
  2. Swipe left or right until you see the Netflix app.
  3. TSwipe up on the Netflix app preview to close it.
    • In iOS 6, tap and hold on the app until a red badge with a line through it appears, then tap the badge to close the app.
  4. Press the Home button to return to the Home screen.
  5. Select Settings.
  6. Swipe down until you locate the Netflix app.
  7. Select Netflix.
  8. Slide the Reset toggle to the On position.

    9. NOTE:Resetting the Netflix app will remove any titles you have downloaded through the app.

  9. Press the Home button to return to the Home screen.
  10. Select Netflix.
  11. Enter your Netflix email address and password.
