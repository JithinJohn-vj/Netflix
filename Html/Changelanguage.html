How to change the language on Netflix


If you want to update your Netflix account information, follow the steps below.

On an iPhone, iPad, Android phone or tablet
  1. From the lower right, tap My Netflix.
  2. From the upper right, tap More or .
  3. Tap Manage Profiles .
  4. Select the profile you want to edit.
  5. Tap Display Language .
  6. Select a language. The new language will automatically save.
On all other devices
  1. From a web browser, go to your Account page.
  2. From the Profile & Parental Controls section, select a profile.
  3. Select Change for Language.
  4. Select a Display Language.
  5. Select Save.
    • If you don't see the language change on the device, you may need to sign out and then sign back in.
On an iPhone, iPad, Android phone or tablet
  1. From the lower right, tap My Netflix.
  2. From the upper right, tap More or .
  3. Tap Manage Profiles .
  4. Select the profile you want to edit.
  5. Tap Audio & Subtitles .
  6. Select preferred languages from Audio & Subtitles. The new languages will automatically save.
On all other devices
  1. From a web browser, go to your Account page.
  2. From the Profile & Parental Controls section, select a profile.
  3. Select Change for Language.
  4. Select preferred languages from Shows & Movies Languages.
  5. Select Save.

NOTE: Netflix shows 5-7 relevant languages on TV and TV-connected devices based on your location and language settings. For iPhone, iPad, Android phones and tablets, and web browsers, you’ll see all available languages for a title. Not all languages are available for all titles.
