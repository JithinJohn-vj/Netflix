How to watch Netflix in 4K Ultra HD


Ultra HD streaming is available on Netflix on many 4K devices.

  • A Netflix plan that supports streaming in Ultra HD.
  • A 60Hz TV or computer monitor compatible with Ultra HD streaming from Netflix.
  • A steady internet connection speed of 15 megabits per second or higher.
  • Streaming quality set to Auto or High.

For device models with Ultra HD available, search the Netflix Help Center for your device. If Ultra HD is available on your device, it will be listed in the Ultra HD section. You can also contact your device manufacturer for more information.

NOTE: Devices must run the latest firmware for Ultra HD playback to work properly.

Netflix is available in Ultra HD on Windows and Mac computers with:

  • Microsoft Edge for Windows
  • Windows app for Windows 10 and Windows 11
  • Safari for MacOS 11.0 or later

These computers require a 4K capable 60Hz monitor to play Ultra HD content.

Search for "4K" or "Ultra HD" in the Netflix app to find TV shows and movies available in 4K Ultra HD on your device. Titles available in 4K Ultra HD will have a 4K icon label next to their description.

NOTE: Most (but not all) TV shows and movies are available in 4K Ultra HD. Some TV shows may not have 4K Ultra HD available for every season.
