Netflix Stories: Love is Blind - Game Support


You're the main character in these interactive story games based on Netflix's
hit shows and films. Romance, drama, intrigue — which fate will you choose?



Platforms: Android phone & tablet, iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch

Game category: Interactive Story

Number of players: Single player

Offline play No

Cloud saves: Yes

Content rating: See the Google Play Store or the App Store

Languages: English (US), French, German, Italian, Portuguese (Brazil), Spanish (Castilian), Spanish (Latin America)

Developer:Boss Fight Entertainment, a Netflix game studio

Requirements

To play, you’ll have to install the game on your device first. You need:

  • A compatible device:
    • Android phone or tablet running Android 12.0 or later with a minimum of 2 GB memory
  • iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch running iOS/iPadOS 15 or later
  • An active Netflix subscription
  • An internet connection to download, install, and play the game
  • Enough storage space on your device

Stuck in the game somewhere? Wondering how something works? Have questions about in-game settings? Here are answers to common gameplay questions.

Possible spoilers below.

  • Changing the language

    • From the landing page, tap SETTINGS. Tap LANGUAGE and choose your language to play in.

  • How to find your Player ID

    • Your Player ID is a unique number associated with your account. This can help our team better find issues if you have to contact support. It can be found in the game by tapping on SETTINGS.

  • Increase player level
    • You gain XP by playing chapters. As your level progresses you'll receive new titles and badges.
  • How to find your Player ID

    • Your Player ID is a unique number associated with your account. This can help our team better find issues if you have to contact support. It can be found in the game by tapping on SETTINGS.

  • Restart Story
    • Restarting the story returns you to the beginning and completely resets your progress. This can be done by tapping the circular arrow button next to the Resume Story or Resume Chapter button.
  • Restart Chapter
    • You can't restart a chapter after you've begun, you may only restart the story.
  • Customize my character in Love is Blind
    • When you begin the story you are able to create a name and choose your pronouns. You can customize your character’s skin tone, body type, eye color, hair color, and hairstyle. You will also pick your outfit and earrings. Once the story begins, you can't change your name or body characteristics.
    • At times during the story, you'll be prompted to change outfits and earrings. You can also click on the hanger icon to change outfits and earrings at any time.

If you don't get any sound or the volume is too quiet:

  • Check your device's volume or sound settings. If your phone has a Silent or Do Not Disturb mode make sure it's off.
  • If the game has its own sound settings, check if they're on and turned up.
