How to watch Netflix on your TV


To watch Netflix on your TV, you need a Device with the Netflix app . The Netflix app is available on many:

  • Smart TVs
  • Set-top boxes
  • Blu-ray players
  • Game consoles

On Netflix-ready TVs, the Netflix app is on the main menu or home screen. If your TV has an app store, search for
Netflix to see if the app is available.

NOTE: Sometimes a device update is needed to find and install the Netflix app. If you're not sure how to update the system
software on your TV, check your owner's manual or contact the manufacturer.

On TVs that don't have the app, you can use a streaming media player or a mobile device to watch Netflix on a TV.
