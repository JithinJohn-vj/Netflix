How to add, change or delete a phone number


Adding a phone number to your account makes sign in easier, improves account security, and can help you get back into your account if you ever forget your password.

To add, change or delete a phone number:

  1. Go to the following link: Change phone number. You will need to sign in if you haven’t already.
  2. Follow the instructions to confirm your identity.
  3. After confirming, you can do the following:
    • Add phone number: choose a country, enter the phone number and then choose Next.
    • Edit: make changes to the phone number and choose Next.
    • Delete: Choose Delete Phone Number.
  4. To use the new number for Netflix you'll need to confirm it's valid by entering the code we sent to your phone in a text message when you changed it. You can also verify it at this link: Confirm your recovery phone number.

NOTE: Only one account can have your verified phone number on it. Verifying your phone number will also remove it from any other accounts it is tied to.

If you aren't getting text messages to the phone number on your account, there could be a delay from your carrier. If the problem continues, please contact your phone carrier for help.
