How to pay for Netflix

Choose a plan that meets your needs and your budget. As a Netflix member, you are charged once a month on the
date you signed up. There are several options to pay for Netflix.

UPI AutoPay

Netflix supports UPI AutoPay through the following consumer apps:

  • Amazon Pay (@apl, @yapl)
  • BHIM (@upi)
  • Google Pay (@okaxis, @okhdfcbank, @okicici, @oksbi)
  • PayTM (@paytm)
  • PhonePe (@axl, @ibl, @ybl)
  • Other (@boi, @cnrb, @dbs, @dlb, @indus, @icici, @sliceaxis, @pz, @ikwik)
